Puppy Playdate

Hello Furiends, lets have a pawfect playdate!

Matching your pup with the right dog is crucial to the success of any playdate. While it might be tempting to invite your best friend to bring their dog over, this might not be a good idea if your two dogs have different personalities and play styles. So take some time to find a dog you think will play well with your pup. It could be the dog of a friend or family member, or maybe a pooch you've previously encountered at your local dog park. Dogs who don't have a great track record of getting along with other canines are obviously a no-no, while it's a good idea to match your pet with a dog of a similar size. Find a calm, friendly, and well-socialized dog, and you'll be making the best possible start.
